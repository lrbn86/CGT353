About

This is a website that lists out the 9 cuisines. Each cuisine has their 9 dishes on the hover images.

This listing is not exhaustive. Some dishes have debatable origins, but they are grouped based on how common they appear with other dishes.

For example, crab rangoon is commonly seen with Chinese cuisine, but its cuisine origin is debatable.


Switch between cuisines by clicking on the arrow keys on the sides or the dots below.

The hover images and dish names should change accordingly.

A popup will appear when clicking on one of the dishes. The popup contains a embedded youtube video of the dish.


* A Youtube video may not be available to be viewed due to copyright or adblocker. If this is the case, please view the video directly on Youtube.

The website is currently not mobile friendly.
